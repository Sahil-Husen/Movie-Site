Dhobi Ghat (2010) Hindi Drama Film

About the Movie

Dhobi Ghat ek 2010 ki Hindi drama film hai jo Kiran Rao ne direct ki hai. Yeh film Mumbai ke alag-alag backgrounds se aaye chaar logon ki kahani batati hai, jinki zindagiyan shehar ke beech anjaane dhagon se judi hoti hain.

Story & Themes

Film loneliness, dreams, art aur urban life ki reality ko ek subtle aur realistic andaaz mein dikhati hai. Mumbai sirf ek jagah nahi balki ek zinda kirdaar ki tarah film ke har frame mein mehsoos hoti hai.
