About the Movie

Dhobi Ghat Mumbai ke alag-alag backgrounds se aaye chaar logon ki kahani hai, jinki zindagiyan ek shehar ke beech anjaane dhagon se judi hoti hain. Film loneliness, dreams, art aur urban life ki reality ko ek subtle aur realistic andaaz mein dikhati hai, jahan Mumbai sirf jagah nahi balki ek zinda kirdaar ban kar ubharti hai.
